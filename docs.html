What is BitNexus?

BitNexus is a decentralized affiliate revenue platform powered by AI. It combines affiliate marketing, MEV bot staking, network building, and automated revenue generation into a single ecosystem.

Quick Start

  1. Sign up for a Genesis account (limited to 500 early adopters)
  2. Purchase NXC tokens from the Token Shop
  3. Explore the Marketplace and select products to promote
  4. Generate affiliate links and start sharing
  5. Stake NXC in MEV bots for passive income
  6. Build your network through the Alliance system

System Requirements

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • JavaScript enabled
  • Internet connection
  • Wallet for cryptocurrency transactions (optional)

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Sign up for a Genesis account, purchase NXC tokens, and start promoting products from the marketplace. Check out our Getting Started guide above for detailed steps.

What are MEV bots?

MEV (Maximal Extractable Value) bots are automated trading systems that generate returns by capturing value from blockchain transactions. You can stake NXC tokens in these bots to earn passive income.

How does the Alliance system work?

The Alliance system is our MLM (Multi-Level Marketing) structure. When you refer new users, they join your network, and you earn commissions from their activity. The larger your network, the more you earn.

Is BitNexus safe?

Yes. We've undergone multiple security audits, use industry-standard encryption, and maintain transparent operations. All smart contracts are audited and publicly verifiable.

Can I withdraw my earnings?

Yes, you can withdraw your NXC earnings to USD or other cryptocurrencies. Withdrawal fees apply, and processing times vary based on the method chosen.
