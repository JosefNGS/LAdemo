## What Are NXC Credits?

**NXC Credits** (Nexus Credits) are the native utility tokens of the BitNexus platform. They serve as the primary currency for platform interactions, AI-powered features, and access to premium services.

### Key Characteristics

- **Utility Token**: Used for platform operations, not a speculative investment
- **Dual Purpose**: Network interactions + AI usage credits
- **Non-Transferable**: Credits are account-bound (in current implementation)
- **Purchasable**: Available in packages from the Credits Shop
- **Earnable**: Can be earned through platform activities

NXC Credits serve two distinct purposes within the BitNexus ecosystem:

### 1. Network Credits (Base NXC)

**Purpose**: Platform interactions and network operations

**Used For**:

**How It Works**:
- Each NXC package includes bonus AI credits
- AI credits are separate from network credits
- Dedicated specifically to AI feature usage
- Cannot be used for network operations

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: What is the difference between NXC Credits and AI Credits?**
A: NXC Credits are used for platform operations (listing fees, staking, transactions). AI Credits are bonus credits included with purchases, dedicated specifically to AI-powered features like Content Generator.

**Q: Can I transfer NXC Credits to another user?**
A: Currently, NXC Credits are account-bound and non-transferable. This may change in future updates.

**Q: Do NXC Credits expire?**
A: No, NXC Credits do not expire. They remain in your account until used.

**Q: What happens if I don't have enough credits?**
A: Features requiring credits will be disabled. You'll see an alert with a "Buy Credits" link to purchase more.

### Pricing Questions

**Q: Why do larger packages have better prices per credit?**
A: Larger packages offer volume discounts to incentivize bigger purchases and provide better value for serious users.

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**
A: No hidden fees. The price shown is the final price. Transaction fees (0.5%) only apply to withdrawals, not purchases.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I don't use my credits?**
A: Refund policies are handled on a case-by-case basis. Contact support for assistance.

### Usage Questions

**Q: How many content generations can I do with 50 AI Credits?**
A: 5 generations (10 AI Credits per generation).

**Q: Can I use AI Credits for product listings?**
A: No, AI Credits are only for AI-powered features. Product listings require Network Credits (NXC).

**Q: What happens to my credits if I cancel my account?**
A: Unused credits are typically forfeited upon account cancellation. Contact support for specific policies.

### Earning Questions

**Q: How fast can I earn credits through affiliate sales?**
A: Depends on your sales volume. With 10 sales/month at $50 commission each, you could earn $500/month, which equals ~167 NXC credits.

**Q: What's the best way to earn credits passively?**
A: Staking in MEV Bot or XAB Bot provides 10-15% APY, earning credits automatically.

**Q: Do I earn credits for referring users?**
A: Yes, referral bonuses are paid in NXC credits. Bonus amounts vary by campaign.
