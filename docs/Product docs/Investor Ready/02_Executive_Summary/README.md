# 02_Executive_Summary

## Purpose
This folder contains high-level business summaries and investment thesis documents.

## Required Documents

### Executive Summary (PDF)
- **Status**: ✅ Available as `Executive_Summary.md` (convert to PDF)
- **Purpose**: 1–2 pages narrative description of the business, market, problem, and solution
- **Content**: Comprehensive overview of BitNexus platform, market opportunity, and value proposition
- **Usage**: First document investors read to understand the business

### Investment Thesis
- **Status**: ⚠️ To be created
- **Purpose**: A brief memo on why this is a good investment now
- **Content**: Market timing, competitive advantages, growth potential, team strength
- **Usage**: Explain why now is the right time to invest

## Notes
- Executive Summary should be concise (1-2 pages maximum)
- Investment Thesis should highlight market timing and opportunity
- Both documents should be updated regularly as the business evolves
