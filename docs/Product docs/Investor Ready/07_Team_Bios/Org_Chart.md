## Hiring Process

### Standard Hiring Process

1. **Job Posting**: Internal and external posting
2. **Resume Screening**: Initial candidate review
3. **Phone Screen**: Initial interview
4. **Technical Assessment**: Skills evaluation (if applicable)
5. **Interview**: Team interviews
6. **Reference Check**: Professional references
7. **Offer**: Job offer and negotiation
8. **Onboarding**: New hire onboarding process

### Hiring Timeline

**Target Timeline**: 4-6 weeks from posting to offer
**Onboarding**: 2-4 weeks integration period
