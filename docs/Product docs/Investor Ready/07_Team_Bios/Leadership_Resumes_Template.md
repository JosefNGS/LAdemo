## Overview

This document provides resume templates and guidelines for creating professional resumes for BitNexus leadership team members. Each team member should create their own resume using these templates and guidelines.

**Note**: Actual resumes should be created as individual PDF files for each team member.

---

## Resume Template Structure

### Section 1: Header

**Format**:
```
[Full Name]
[Title/Role]
[Email] | [Phone] | [LinkedIn] | [Website (if applicable)]
```

**Example**:
```
Josef Lindbom
COO & Development Vision Lead
josef@nordicglobalsolutions.com | [Phone] | [LinkedIn] | www.nordicglobalsolutions.com
```

---

### Section 2: Professional Summary

**Purpose**: Brief overview of professional background and expertise

**Format**:
- 3-4 sentences
- Highlight key achievements
- Emphasize relevant experience
- Show value proposition

**Example Template**:
```
[Name] is a [role] with [X] years of experience in [industry/field]. 
[Key achievement or expertise]. [Relevant experience or skills]. 
Currently serving as [current role] at BitNexus, [key contribution or focus].
```

---

### Section 3: Professional Experience

**Format**:
```
[Company Name] | [Location]
[Job Title] | [Start Date] - [End Date / Present]

• [Achievement or responsibility]
• [Achievement or responsibility]
• [Achievement or responsibility]
```

**Guidelines**:
- List most recent positions first
- Use bullet points for achievements
- Quantify results when possible
- Focus on relevant experience
- Highlight leadership and impact

---

### Section 4: Education

**Format**:
```
[Degree], [Field of Study]
[University Name] | [Location]
[Graduation Year]
[Honors or relevant details (optional)]
```

**Guidelines**:
- List highest degree first
- Include relevant certifications
- Add professional development courses if relevant

---

### Section 5: Skills & Expertise

**Format**:
- **Technical Skills**: [List relevant technical skills]
- **Business Skills**: [List relevant business skills]
- **Industry Expertise**: [List relevant industry knowledge]

**Guidelines**:
- Focus on skills relevant to role
- Group by category
- Be specific and accurate
- Include both hard and soft skills

---

### Section 6: Achievements & Awards (Optional)

**Format**:
- [Achievement or award]
- [Achievement or award]
- [Achievement or award]

**Guidelines**:
- Include significant achievements
- Professional awards or recognition
- Publications or speaking engagements
- Relevant certifications

## Resume Best Practices

### Do's

✅ **Use Professional Formatting**:
- Clean, easy-to-read layout
- Consistent formatting
- Professional fonts
- Proper spacing

✅ **Quantify Achievements**:
- Use numbers and metrics
- Show impact and results
- Demonstrate value

✅ **Tailor to Audience**:
- Focus on relevant experience
- Highlight relevant skills
- Emphasize relevant achievements

✅ **Keep It Concise**:
- 1-2 pages maximum
- Focus on most relevant information
- Remove outdated information

✅ **Proofread Carefully**:
- No spelling or grammar errors
- Consistent formatting
- Accurate information

---

### Don'ts

❌ **Don't Include Personal Information**:
- Age, marital status, photos (unless required)
- Personal hobbies (unless relevant)
- Political or religious affiliations

❌ **Don't Exaggerate**:
- Be honest about experience
- Accurate dates and titles
- Realistic achievements

❌ **Don't Use Unprofessional Language**:
- Avoid slang or casual language
- Use professional terminology
- Proper grammar and spelling

❌ **Don't Include Irrelevant Information**:
- Focus on relevant experience
- Remove outdated skills
- Keep it focused
