## Overview

This document provides a template for Advisory Agreements. The actual agreement should be created with legal counsel and tailored to the specific needs of BitNexus and each advisor.

**⚠️ IMPORTANT**: This is a template only. Actual advisory agreements must be created with qualified legal counsel and comply with applicable laws in the relevant jurisdiction.

---

## Advisory Agreement Template

### Standard Structure

```
ADVISORY AGREEMENT

This Advisory Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [Date] 
("Effective Date") between BitNexus, Inc. (the "Company") and 
[Advisor Name] (the "Advisor").

1. ADVISORY SERVICES
   Advisor agrees to provide advisory services as described in 
   Exhibit A attached hereto.

2. TERM
   This Agreement shall commence on [Date] and continue for [Term] 
   unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Agreement.

3. COMPENSATION
   Company shall compensate Advisor as set forth in Section 3.

4. EQUITY COMPENSATION
   Advisor shall receive equity compensation as set forth in Section 4.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
   Advisor agrees to maintain confidentiality of Company information.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
   Advisor assigns all IP created during advisory period to Company.

7. NON-COMPETE (if applicable)
   [Non-compete provisions]

8. TERMINATION
   Either party may terminate this Agreement [with/without] cause 
   upon [X] days written notice.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement.

COMPANY:                          ADVISOR:
_________________                 _________________
[Company Representative]          [Advisor Name]
```

## Key Provisions

### 1. Advisory Services

**Standard Services Clause**:

```
ADVISORY SERVICES

Advisor agrees to provide the following advisory services:

(a) Strategic guidance and advice
(b) Industry insights and market intelligence
(c) Network introductions and connections
(d) Mentorship and coaching
(e) Participation in advisory meetings ([X] per [period])
(f) Availability for consultation ([X] hours per [period])

Specific services are detailed in Exhibit A.
```

---

### 2. Compensation Structure

**Standard Compensation Clause**:

```
COMPENSATION

Company shall compensate Advisor as follows:

(a) Equity Compensation: [X]% of total equity (or [X] shares)
(b) Cash Compensation: $[Amount] per [period] (if applicable)
(c) Expenses: Reimbursement of reasonable expenses
(d) Other: [Other compensation]

Equity Compensation:
- Vesting Schedule: [X] years, [X]% per year
- Cliff: [X] months
- Acceleration: [If applicable]
```

---

### 3. Equity Compensation

**Standard Equity Clause**:

```
EQUITY COMPENSATION

Advisor shall receive [X]% of total equity (or [X] shares of 
Common Stock) as compensation for advisory services.

Vesting Schedule:
- Vesting Period: [X] years
- Cliff Period: [X] months
- Monthly Vesting: [X]% per month after cliff
- Acceleration: [If applicable - e.g., acquisition]

Equity Type: [Stock Options / Restricted Stock / Other]
Exercise Price: $[Amount] per share (if options)
```

---

### 4. Confidentiality

**Standard Confidentiality Clause**:

```
CONFIDENTIALITY

Advisor agrees to hold in strict confidence and not to disclose, 
use, or exploit any Confidential Information of Company.

"Confidential Information" includes:
- Business plans and strategies
- Financial information
- Technical information
- Customer information
- Any other proprietary information

This obligation shall survive termination of this Agreement.
```

---

### 5. Intellectual Property

**Standard IP Clause**:

```
INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Advisor hereby assigns to Company all right, title, and interest 
in and to any inventions, discoveries, improvements, works of 
authorship, and other intellectual property that Advisor makes, 
conceives, or reduces to practice during the term of this Agreement 
that relate to Company's business.

Advisor agrees to assist Company in obtaining and enforcing 
intellectual property rights.
```

---

### 6. Non-Compete (If Applicable)

**Standard Non-Compete Clause**:

```
NON-COMPETE

During the term of this Agreement and for [X] months/years after 
termination, Advisor agrees not to:

(a) Engage in any business that competes with Company's business
(b) Solicit Company's customers or employees
(c) Use or disclose Company's confidential information

This provision is limited to [geographic area] and [specific activities].

Note: Non-compete clauses must comply with applicable laws and may 
not be enforceable in all jurisdictions.
```
