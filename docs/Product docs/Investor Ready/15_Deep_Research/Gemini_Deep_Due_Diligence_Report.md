### 3.3 Blockchain Infrastructure and Security

**Network Deployment**:
- **Primary Layer**: To support high-frequency affiliate payouts and staking interactions, the system likely operates on Ethereum Layer 2 (Base) or Solana.

## 6. Regulatory Compliance Framework: The "Finland 2026" Roadmap

Operating a crypto-affiliate system from Finland in 2026 requires navigating one of the world's most rigorous regulatory environments. This section populates the Legal & Compliance folder.

### 6.1 MiCA Authorization (Mandatory)

Since the expiration of the transitional period in mid-2025, no entity can offer crypto services in Finland without authorization.

**Requirement**: BitNexus must be authorized as a Crypto-Asset Service Provider (CASP) by the Finnish Financial Supervisory Authority (FIN-FSA).

**Process**:
1. Establish a Finnish legal entity with physical presence (e.g., utilizing the Inari office of Jonne Waselius).
2. Demonstrate "Fit and Proper" management (Leverage Svein Nilsen's board experience).
3. Implement segregation of client funds and robust IT security protocols.

**Risk**: Operating without this authorization is a criminal offense and will lead to immediate shutdown.

### 6.2 Taxation and Reporting (CARF & DAC8)

Starting January 1, 2026, Finland enforces the Crypto-Asset Reporting Framework (CARF).

**Obligation**: BitNexus must collect extensive data on all users (tax IDs, residence) and report granular transaction details (transfers, exchanges) to the Finnish Tax Administration.

**Deadline**: First annual reports are due in 2027 covering the 2026 tax year.

**System Requirement**: The technical stack must include a "Tax Engine" that logs every micro-transaction in a CARF-compliant format.

### 6.3 Consumer Protection (KKV) & Marketing

The Finnish Competition and Consumer Authority (KKV) strictly regulates marketing practices.

**Transparency**: All affiliate links generated by the system must be clearly labeled as advertisements (e.g., "Mainos"). Hidden advertising is illegal.

**Anti-Scam Measures**: Marketing materials must rigorously avoid "Get Rich Quick" language. Given the history of "BitNexus" HYIP scams, the project must proactively demonstrate that it does not offer guaranteed returns.

**Direct Marketing**: The system must respect Finnish "Do Not Call/Email" registries.
