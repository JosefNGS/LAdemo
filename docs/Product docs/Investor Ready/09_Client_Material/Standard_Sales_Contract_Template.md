## Overview

This document provides a template for the standard Master Services Agreement (MSA) used for BitNexus sales. The actual contract should be created with legal counsel and tailored to specific customer needs and jurisdictions.

**⚠️ IMPORTANT**: This is a template only. Actual sales contracts must be created with qualified legal counsel and comply with applicable laws in the relevant jurisdiction.

---

## Master Services Agreement Template

### Standard Structure

```
MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [Date] 
("Effective Date") between BitNexus, Inc. ("Company") and [Customer Name] 
("Customer").

1. SERVICES
   Company agrees to provide the services described in Exhibit A attached 
   hereto (the "Services").

2. TERM
   This Agreement shall commence on [Date] and continue for [Term] unless 
   earlier terminated in accordance with this Agreement.

3. FEES AND PAYMENT
   Customer shall pay Company the fees set forth in Exhibit B attached 
   hereto. Payment terms: [Net 30/Net 15/etc.].

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
   Company retains all rights in the Services and related IP. Customer 
   receives a license to use the Services as provided herein.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
   Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of each other's 
   confidential information.

6. WARRANTIES AND DISCLAIMERS
   [Standard warranty and disclaimer provisions]

7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
   [Limitation of liability provisions]

8. TERMINATION
   Either party may terminate this Agreement [with/without] cause upon 
   [X] days written notice.

9. GOVERNING LAW
   This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of [Jurisdiction].

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement.

COMPANY:                          CUSTOMER:
_________________                 _________________
[Company Representative]          [Customer Representative]
```

## Key Provisions

### 1. Services Description

**Standard Services Clause**:

```
SERVICES

Company shall provide Customer with access to the BitNexus platform, 
including:

(a) Affiliate Marketing Tools
(b) Passive Staking Services (MEV Bot, XAB Bot)
(c) Network Building Tools
(d) AI Content Generation
(e) Analytics and Reporting
(f) Customer Support

Specific service levels and features are set forth in Exhibit A.
```

---

### 2. Pricing Structure

**Standard Pricing Clause**:

```
FEES AND PAYMENT

Customer shall pay Company the following fees:

(a) Subscription Fee: $[Amount] per [month/year]
(b) Transaction Fees: [X]% of [transaction type]
(c) Additional Services: As set forth in Exhibit B

Payment Terms: Net [X] days from invoice date.
Late Payment: [X]% interest per month on overdue amounts.
```

---

### 3. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

**Standard SLA Provisions**:

```
SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

Uptime Commitment: Company shall maintain [99.9%] uptime.

Support Response Times:
- Critical Issues: [X] hours
- High Priority: [X] hours
- Standard: [X] hours

Service Credits: [Description of service credits for SLA breaches]
```

---

### 4. Data and Privacy

**Standard Data Provisions**:

```
DATA AND PRIVACY

Customer Data: Customer retains ownership of all customer data.

Data Processing: Company processes customer data in accordance with 
applicable privacy laws and Company's privacy policy.

Security: Company maintains industry-standard security measures.

Data Retention: [Data retention policies]
```

---

### 5. Intellectual Property

**Standard IP Provisions**:

```
INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Company IP: Company retains all rights in the Services, platform, and 
related intellectual property.

Customer License: Customer receives a limited, non-exclusive, 
non-transferable license to use the Services during the Term.

Customer IP: Customer retains all rights in customer data and content.
```
