# 09_Client_Material

## Purpose
This folder contains customer success stories, contracts, and pipeline information.

## Required Documents

### Case Studies
- **Status**: ⚠️ To be created
- **Purpose**: Success stories with metrics
- **Content**: 
  - Customer success stories
  - Quantified results and ROI
  - Testimonials and quotes
  - Before/after comparisons
- **Usage**: Demonstrate product value and customer success

### Standard Sales Contract
- **Status**: ⚠️ To be created
- **Purpose**: The boilerplate MSA (Master Services Agreement)
- **Content**: 
  - Standard terms and conditions
  - Pricing structure
  - Service level agreements
  - Legal terms
- **Usage**: Show contract structure and terms

### Testimonials/References
- **Status**: ⚠️ To be created
- **Purpose**: List of clients willing to speak to investors
- **Content**: 
  - Customer testimonials
  - Reference contact information
  - Permission to contact
- **Usage**: Enable investor reference checks

### Pipeline Report
- **Status**: ⚠️ To be created
- **Purpose**: Anonymized list of potential deals in the funnel
- **Content**: 
  - Sales pipeline overview
  - Deal stages and probabilities
  - Expected close dates
  - Deal values
- **Usage**: Show sales momentum and opportunity

## Notes
- Case studies should include specific, measurable results
- Contracts should be reviewed by legal counsel
- Testimonials should be authentic and verifiable
- Pipeline should be kept current and realistic
