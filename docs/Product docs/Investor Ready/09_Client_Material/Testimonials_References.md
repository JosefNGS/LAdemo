## Testimonial Best Practices

### Do's

✅ **Use Real Testimonials**:
- Authentic customer quotes
- Verifiable results
- Real customer names (with permission)
- Specific achievements

✅ **Get Permission**:
- Written permission to use
- Approval of final text
- Right to modify/update
- Usage rights clarification

✅ **Keep Updated**:
- Regular testimonial collection
- Update with new successes
- Refresh old testimonials
- Maintain relevance

---

### Don'ts

❌ **Don't Fabricate**:
- No fake testimonials
- No exaggerated claims
- No false results
- No misleading information

❌ **Don't Use Without Permission**:
- Always get customer approval
- Respect privacy preferences
- Follow usage restrictions
- Maintain confidentiality
