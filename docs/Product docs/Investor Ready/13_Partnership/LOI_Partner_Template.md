## Overview

This document provides a template for a Letter of Intent (LOI) for partnership engagements. The LOI is typically a non-binding document that outlines the proposed terms of a partnership before entering into a formal Partnership Agreement.

**⚠️ IMPORTANT**: This is a template only. Actual LOIs must be created with legal counsel and tailored to specific partnership engagements. LOIs are typically non-binding but may contain binding provisions (e.g., exclusivity, confidentiality).

## Letter of Intent Template

### Standard Format

```
LETTER OF INTENT

Date: [Date]

To: [Partner Name]
[Partner Address]

From: BitNexus, Inc.
[Company Address]

Subject: Letter of Intent for Strategic Partnership

Dear [Partner Name],

This Letter of Intent ("LOI") sets forth the proposed terms and 
conditions for a strategic partnership between BitNexus, Inc. 
("Company") and [Partner Name] ("Partner").

1. PROPOSED PARTNERSHIP
   [Description of proposed partnership]

2. PROPOSED PARTNERSHIP TYPE
   [Type of partnership: Distribution, Technology, Strategic, Channel]

3. PROPOSED TERM
   [Proposed term of partnership]

4. PROPOSED TERMS
   [Proposed partnership terms]

5. PROPOSED TIMELINE
   [Proposed timeline and milestones]

6. BINDING PROVISIONS
   [Provisions that are binding, if any]

7. NON-BINDING PROVISIONS
   [Provisions that are non-binding]

8. EXCLUSIVITY (if applicable)
   [Exclusivity provisions]

9. CONFIDENTIALITY
   [Confidentiality provisions]

10. TERMINATION
    [Termination provisions]

11. GOVERNING LAW
    [Governing law and jurisdiction]

This LOI is intended to facilitate negotiations and does not create 
a binding agreement except as specifically set forth herein.

Sincerely,

BitNexus, Inc.

By: _________________
[Name], [Title]

ACCEPTED:

[Partner Name]

By: _________________
[Name], [Title]

Date: [Date]
```

## Key Provisions

### 1. Proposed Partnership

**Standard Partnership Clause**:

```
PROPOSED PARTNERSHIP

Company and Partner propose to enter into a strategic partnership 
for the following purposes:

(a) [Partnership Objective 1]: [Description]
(b) [Partnership Objective 2]: [Description]
(c) [Partnership Objective 3]: [Description]

Specific partnership terms, responsibilities, and benefits will be 
detailed in the final Partnership Agreement.
```

---

### 2. Partnership Type

**Standard Partnership Type Clause**:

```
PROPOSED PARTNERSHIP TYPE

This partnership is proposed as a [Type] partnership:

Distribution Partnership:
- Partner will distribute/resell Company's products/services
- Revenue sharing: [X]% to Partner, [X]% to Company
- Territory: [Geographic scope]

Technology Partnership:
- Integration of Company and Partner technologies
- Joint development opportunities
- Technology licensing

Strategic Partnership:
- Strategic alignment and collaboration
- Co-marketing opportunities
- Market access and expansion

Channel Partnership:
- Partner referral program
- Commission structure: [X]% per referral
- Partner enablement and support
```

---

### 3. Proposed Terms

**Standard Terms Clause**:

```
PROPOSED TERMS

Company and Partner propose the following partnership terms:

Revenue Sharing:
- Partner receives: [X]% of [revenue type]
- Company receives: [X]% of [revenue type]
- Payment terms: [Net 30/Net 15/etc.]

Responsibilities:
- Company: [Company responsibilities]
- Partner: [Partner responsibilities]

Benefits:
- Company: [Company benefits]
- Partner: [Partner benefits]

Final terms will be confirmed in the Partnership Agreement.
```

---

### 4. Proposed Timeline

**Standard Timeline Clause**:

```
PROPOSED TIMELINE

Proposed partnership timeline:

- LOI Execution: [Date]
- Partnership Agreement Negotiation: [X] days
- Partnership Agreement Execution: [Target Date]
- Partnership Launch: [Target Date]
- Initial Term: [X] months/years
- Renewal: [Automatic/By mutual agreement]

Timeline is subject to Partnership Agreement negotiation and execution.
```

---

### 5. Binding Provisions

**Standard Binding Provisions**:

```
BINDING PROVISIONS

The following provisions are binding and enforceable:

(a) Confidentiality: Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality
(b) Exclusivity: [If applicable - exclusivity period]
(c) Good Faith Negotiation: Both parties agree to negotiate in good faith
(d) Expenses: [If applicable - expense reimbursement]

All other provisions are non-binding and subject to Partnership 
Agreement negotiation.
```

---

### 6. Non-Binding Provisions

**Standard Non-Binding Clause**:

```
NON-BINDING PROVISIONS

Except as specifically set forth in the "Binding Provisions" section, 
this LOI is non-binding and does not create any legal obligation. 
The parties intend to enter into a formal Partnership Agreement that 
will contain the definitive terms and conditions.

This LOI is for discussion purposes only and does not constitute 
an offer or acceptance of an offer.
```

---

### 7. Exclusivity (If Applicable)

**Standard Exclusivity Clause**:

```
EXCLUSIVITY

During the period from [Date] to [Date] (the "Exclusivity Period"):

(a) Partner agrees to negotiate exclusively with Company
(b) Partner will not engage with competing partners
(c) Exclusivity applies to [specific partnership scope/territory]

If Partnership Agreement is not executed by [Date], exclusivity expires.

This provision is binding and enforceable.
```

---

### 8. Confidentiality

**Standard Confidentiality Clause**:

```
CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of all 
information disclosed in connection with this LOI and the 
proposed partnership.

Confidential Information includes:
- Business plans and strategies
- Financial information
- Technical information
- Customer information
- Partnership terms and discussions
- Any other proprietary information

This obligation survives termination of this LOI.

This provision is binding and enforceable.
```

---

### 9. Termination

**Standard Termination Clause**:

```
TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this LOI:

(a) At any time with [X] days written notice
(b) Immediately if the other party breaches binding provisions
(c) Automatically if Partnership Agreement is not executed by [Date]

Upon termination, binding provisions (confidentiality, etc.) 
shall survive.
```

## LOI vs. Partnership Agreement

### Purpose of LOI

**LOI Purpose**:
- Outline proposed partnership terms
- Facilitate negotiations
- Demonstrate commitment
- Set expectations
- Non-binding (except specified provisions)

**Partnership Agreement Purpose**:
- Definitive partnership contract
- Binding legal agreement
- Detailed terms and conditions
- Rights and obligations
- Full legal protection

## Best Practices

### LOI Best Practices

**Do's**:
- ✅ Clearly mark binding vs. non-binding provisions
- ✅ Include confidentiality provisions
- ✅ Set realistic timelines
- ✅ Be specific about proposed terms
- ✅ Include termination provisions
- ✅ Define partnership type clearly

**Don'ts**:
- ❌ Don't create unintended binding obligations
- ❌ Don't make commitments you can't keep
- ❌ Don't skip legal review
- ❌ Don't use vague language
- ❌ Don't forget expiration dates

---

## Legal Considerations

### Legal Review

**Required Legal Review**:
- Review by qualified attorney
- Compliance with applicable laws
- Clear binding vs. non-binding language
- Proper execution and signatures

**Key Legal Points**:
- LOIs are typically non-binding
- Binding provisions must be clearly stated
- Confidentiality is usually binding
- Exclusivity (if included) is usually binding
- Good faith negotiation may be binding
