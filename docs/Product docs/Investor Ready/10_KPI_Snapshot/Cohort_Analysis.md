## Overview

Cohort analysis tracks how groups of users (cohorts) behave over time. This analysis helps identify retention patterns, revenue trends, and user value by cohort. Cohorts are typically defined by sign-up month or acquisition channel.

**Update Frequency**: Monthly for comprehensive analysis

---

## Cohort Definition

### Cohort Types

**Sign-up Month Cohorts**:
- Users grouped by month they signed up
- Example: January 2026 cohort, February 2026 cohort

**Acquisition Channel Cohorts**:
- Users grouped by how they were acquired
- Example: Network Marketing cohort, Content Marketing cohort

**Plan Type Cohorts**:
- Users grouped by subscription plan
- Example: Starter cohort, Growth cohort, Pro cohort
