## Overview

This document provides templates for standard employee agreements, including IP assignment clauses, confidentiality agreements, and employment terms. Actual agreements should be created with legal counsel and tailored to specific roles and jurisdictions.

**⚠️ IMPORTANT**: These are templates only. Actual employment agreements must be created with qualified legal counsel and comply with applicable employment laws in the relevant jurisdiction.

## Employment Agreement Template

### Standard Format

```
EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [Date] 
("Effective Date") between BitNexus, Inc. (the "Company") and [Employee Name] 
(the "Employee").

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
   Employee shall serve as [Title] and shall perform such duties as assigned 
   by the Company. Employee shall devote full time and attention to Company 
   business.

2. COMPENSATION
   a. Base Salary: $[Amount] per [period]
   b. Equity: [Description of equity/options]
   c. Benefits: [Description of benefits]

3. TERM
   This Agreement shall commence on [Date] and continue until terminated 
   in accordance with this Agreement.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
   Employee agrees to maintain confidentiality of Company information.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
   Employee assigns all IP created during employment to Company.

6. NON-COMPETE (if applicable)
   [Non-compete provisions]

7. TERMINATION
   [Termination provisions]

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement.

COMPANY:                          EMPLOYEE:
_________________                 _________________
[Company Representative]          [Employee Name]
```

## Key Provisions

### 1. Intellectual Property Assignment

**Standard IP Assignment Clause**:

```
INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ASSIGNMENT

Employee hereby assigns to Company all right, title, and interest in and to 
any and all inventions, discoveries, improvements, works of authorship, 
and other intellectual property (collectively, "IP") that Employee makes, 
conceives, or reduces to practice, either alone or jointly with others, 
during the term of employment that:

(a) relate to Company's business or actual or anticipated research or 
    development;
(b) result from any work performed by Employee for Company; or
(c) are developed using Company's equipment, supplies, facilities, or 
    confidential information.

Employee agrees to assist Company in obtaining and enforcing patents, 
copyrights, and other rights in such IP.
```

---

### 2. Confidentiality Agreement

**Standard Confidentiality Clause**:

```
CONFIDENTIALITY

Employee agrees to hold in strict confidence and not to disclose, use, or 
exploit any Confidential Information of Company. "Confidential Information" 
includes, but is not limited to:

- Technical information, know-how, and trade secrets
- Business plans, strategies, and financial information
- Customer lists and relationships
- Product plans and roadmaps
- Marketing strategies and plans
- Any other proprietary information

This obligation shall survive termination of employment.
```

---

### 3. Non-Compete Clause (If Applicable)

**Standard Non-Compete Clause**:

```
NON-COMPETE

During employment and for [X] months/years after termination, Employee 
agrees not to:

(a) engage in any business that competes with Company's business;
(b) solicit Company's customers or employees;
(c) use or disclose Company's confidential information.

This provision is limited to [geographic area] and [specific activities].
```

**Note**: Non-compete clauses must comply with applicable laws and may not be enforceable in all jurisdictions.

---

### 4. Non-Solicitation Clause

**Standard Non-Solicitation Clause**:

```
NON-SOLICITATION

During employment and for [X] months/years after termination, Employee 
agrees not to:

(a) solicit, recruit, or hire Company employees;
(b) solicit or induce Company customers to terminate relationships;
(c) interfere with Company's business relationships.
```
