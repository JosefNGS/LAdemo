## Key Provisions

### 1. Share Transfer Restrictions

**Standard Transfer Restriction Clause**:

```
SHARE TRANSFER RESTRICTIONS

No Shareholder may Transfer any Shares without:
(a) First offering the Shares to the Company and other Shareholders
(b) Complying with right of first refusal procedures
(c) Obtaining required consents
(d) Complying with applicable securities laws

"Transfer" includes any sale, assignment, pledge, or other disposition.
```

---

### 2. Right of First Refusal

**Standard ROFR Clause**:

```
RIGHT OF FIRST REFUSAL

If a Shareholder proposes to Transfer Shares, the Company and other 
Shareholders shall have the right to purchase such Shares on the same 
terms and conditions.

Procedure:
1. Shareholder provides written notice of proposed Transfer
2. Company and Shareholders have [X] days to exercise right
3. Purchase at proposed price and terms
4. If not exercised, Shareholder may proceed with Transfer
```

---

### 3. Tag-Along Rights

**Standard Tag-Along Clause**:

```
TAG-ALONG RIGHTS

If a Majority Shareholder proposes to Transfer Shares to a third party, 
Minority Shareholders have the right to participate in such Transfer 
on a pro-rata basis.

Procedure:
1. Majority Shareholder provides notice of proposed Transfer
2. Minority Shareholders may elect to participate
3. Transfer includes both Majority and participating Minority Shares
4. Same price and terms for all participating Shareholders
```

---

### 4. Drag-Along Rights

**Standard Drag-Along Clause**:

```
DRAG-ALONG RIGHTS

If Majority Shareholders propose to sell the Company, they may require 
Minority Shareholders to participate in such sale.

Procedure:
1. Majority Shareholders provide notice of proposed sale
2. Minority Shareholders must participate in sale
3. Same price and terms for all Shareholders
4. Minority Shareholders execute necessary documents
```

---

### 5. Board Representation

**Standard Board Representation Clause**:

```
BOARD REPRESENTATION

Shareholders with [X]% or more ownership shall have the right to 
designate [X] director(s) to the Board of Directors.

Board composition:
- [X] directors designated by [Shareholder Group]
- [X] directors designated by [Shareholder Group]
- [X] independent directors
```

---

### 6. Voting Agreements

**Standard Voting Agreement Clause**:

```
VOTING AGREEMENTS

Shareholders agree to vote their Shares on certain matters:

Required Supermajority (e.g., 75%):
- Sale of Company
- Merger or acquisition
- Issuance of new shares
- Amendment of Articles of Incorporation
- Liquidation or dissolution

Required Majority (e.g., 50%):
- Election of directors
- Approval of annual budget
- Major contracts
- Strategic decisions
```

---

### 7. Information Rights

**Standard Information Rights Clause**:

```
INFORMATION RIGHTS

Shareholders with [X]% or more ownership shall have the right to:

(a) Access to financial statements (monthly, quarterly, annual)
(b) Access to board meeting minutes
(c) Access to material contracts
(d) Access to cap table
(e) Annual shareholder meeting
(f) Right to inspect books and records
```

---

### 8. Liquidation Preferences

**Standard Liquidation Preference Clause**:

```
LIQUIDATION PREFERENCES

In the event of liquidation, merger, or sale:

Preferred Stock (if applicable):
- Liquidation preference: [1x, 1.5x, 2x] of original investment
- Participation rights: [Participating/Non-participating]
- Conversion rights: [As specified]

Common Stock:
- Pro-rata distribution after Preferred Stock preferences
```
