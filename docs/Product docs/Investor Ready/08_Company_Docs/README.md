# 08_Company_Docs

## Purpose
This folder contains legal and corporate governance documents.

## Required Documents

### Certificate of Incorporation
- **Status**: ⚠️ To be created
- **Purpose**: Legal proof of company existence
- **Content**: Official incorporation documents
- **Usage**: Verify legal entity status

### Bylaws / Operating Agreement
- **Status**: ⚠️ To be created
- **Purpose**: Corporate governance rules
- **Content**: 
  - Corporate bylaws (for corporations)
  - Operating agreement (for LLCs)
  - Governance structure
  - Decision-making processes
- **Usage**: Show corporate governance framework

### Board Consents/Minutes
- **Status**: ⚠️ To be created
- **Purpose**: Records of major company decisions
- **Content**: 
  - Board meeting minutes
  - Written consents
  - Major decisions and approvals
- **Usage**: Demonstrate proper corporate governance

### Standard Employee Agreements
- **Status**: ⚠️ To be created
- **Purpose**: Sample IP assignment and employment contracts
- **Content**: 
  - Employment agreement template
  - IP assignment clauses
  - Confidentiality agreements
  - Non-compete clauses (if applicable)
- **Usage**: Show IP protection and employment practices

## Notes
- All documents should be reviewed by legal counsel
- Keep documents organized and easily accessible
- Ensure compliance with local corporate laws
- Cap Table may also be placed here if preferred over `03_Financial_Model`
