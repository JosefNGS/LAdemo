# Board Consents & Minutes Template
## Records of Major Company Decisions

**Date**: January 2026  
**Version**: 1.0  
**Status**: Template - To be used for actual board meetings  
**Purpose**: Document board decisions and corporate governance

---

## Overview

This document provides templates for board meeting minutes and written consents. These documents serve as official records of major company decisions and demonstrate proper corporate governance.

**⚠️ IMPORTANT**: Actual board minutes and consents should be created with legal counsel and comply with applicable corporate laws.

---

## Board Meeting Minutes Template

### Standard Format

```
BITNEXUS, INC.
BOARD OF DIRECTORS MEETING MINUTES

Date: [Date]
Time: [Time]
Location: [Location or Video Conference]

ATTENDANCE:
Present:
- [Director Name], [Title]
- [Director Name], [Title]
- [Director Name], [Title]

Absent:
- [Director Name], [Title] (excused/unexcused)

Also Present:
- [Officer Name], [Title]
- [Other Attendees]

QUORUM:
A quorum was present. [X] of [Y] directors were present.

CALL TO ORDER:
The meeting was called to order at [time] by [Chairperson Name].

APPROVAL OF PREVIOUS MINUTES:
The minutes of the previous meeting held on [date] were reviewed and approved.

AGENDA ITEMS:

1. [Agenda Item 1]
   Discussion: [Summary of discussion]
   Action: [Action taken]
   Vote: [Vote results - For: X, Against: Y, Abstain: Z]
   Resolution: [Resolution passed/failed]

2. [Agenda Item 2]
   Discussion: [Summary of discussion]
   Action: [Action taken]
   Vote: [Vote results]
   Resolution: [Resolution passed/failed]

3. [Agenda Item 3]
   Discussion: [Summary of discussion]
   Action: [Action taken]
   Vote: [Vote results]
   Resolution: [Resolution passed/failed]

OTHER BUSINESS:
[Any other business discussed]

ADJOURNMENT:
The meeting was adjourned at [time].

Respectfully submitted,

[Secretary Name]
Secretary

Date: [Date]
```

## Written Consent Template

### Standard Format

```
BITNEXUS, INC.
WRITTEN CONSENT OF BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Date: [Date]

The undersigned, being all of the directors of BitNexus, Inc. (the "Corporation"), 
hereby consent to the following actions without a meeting:

RESOLVED, that [description of action/resolution];

RESOLVED, that [description of action/resolution];

RESOLVED, that [description of action/resolution];

This consent may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an 
original, and all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the undersigned have executed this written consent as of 
the date first written above.

[Director Name]                    [Director Name]
_________________                  _________________
[Director Name]                    [Director Name]
_________________                  _________________
```

## Common Board Resolutions

### Resolution Types

**1. Corporate Actions**:
- Approval of annual budget
- Approval of major contracts
- Approval of equity issuances
- Approval of mergers/acquisitions

**2. Officer Appointments**:
- Appointment of officers
- Compensation approvals
- Role changes

**3. Financial Matters**:
- Bank account openings
- Loan approvals
- Investment decisions
- Financial reporting

**4. Operational Matters**:
- Strategic initiatives
- Product launches
- Partnership agreements
- Major operational changes

## Meeting Frequency

### Regular Meetings

**Annual Meeting**: Required annual meeting
**Quarterly Meetings**: Regular quarterly board meetings
**Special Meetings**: As needed for urgent matters

### Written Consents

**When to Use**:
- Routine matters
- Unanimous consent
- Between regular meetings
- Non-controversial items

---

## Documentation Requirements

### Required Information

**Meeting Minutes Must Include**:
- Date, time, location
- Attendees (present and absent)
- Quorum confirmation
- Agenda items
- Discussion summaries
- Actions taken
- Vote results
- Resolutions passed

**Written Consents Must Include**:
- Date of consent
- All directors' signatures
- Clear description of actions
- Unanimous consent confirmation

### Document Storage
