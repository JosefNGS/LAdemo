## Key Provisions to Include

### Governance Provisions

- **Board Structure**: Number of directors, election process
- **Voting Rights**: Voting procedures, quorum requirements
- **Decision Making**: Major decisions requiring board approval
- **Officer Roles**: Duties and responsibilities of officers

### Equity Provisions

- **Stock/Interest Classes**: Common, preferred, options
- **Vesting**: Vesting schedules for equity
- **Transfer Restrictions**: Restrictions on transferring equity
- **Dilution Protection**: Anti-dilution provisions (if applicable)

### Financial Provisions

- **Capital Contributions**: Initial and additional contributions
- **Distributions**: Profit distribution policies
- **Financial Reporting**: Financial reporting requirements

### Legal Provisions

- **Indemnification**: Director and officer indemnification
- **Liability**: Limitation of liability
- **Dispute Resolution**: Dispute resolution procedures
- **Governing Law**: Applicable law and jurisdiction

## Legal Review Requirements

### Required Legal Review

**Before Adoption**:
- Review by qualified corporate attorney
- Compliance with applicable laws
- Tailoring to company needs
- Approval by board/members

**After Adoption**:
- Regular review and updates
- Compliance monitoring
- Amendment process
- Legal updates

## Maintenance

### Regular Updates

**Update Triggers**:
- Changes in corporate structure
- New investors or equity transactions
- Regulatory changes
- Business model changes

**Review Schedule**:
- **Annually**: Annual review
- **As Needed**: When changes occur
- **Before Major Transactions**: Before fundraising, M&A, etc.
