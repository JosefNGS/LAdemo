## Incorporation Process

### Step 1: Choose Jurisdiction

**Considerations**:
- Tax implications
- Regulatory requirements
- Business operations location
- Investor preferences
- Legal framework

**Common Jurisdictions**:
- Delaware (US) - Common for startups
- Finland - EU operations
- Other jurisdictions as appropriate

---

### Step 2: Name Reservation

**Requirements**:
- Check name availability
- Reserve company name
- Ensure name compliance
- File name reservation (if required)

---

### Step 3: Prepare Incorporation Documents

**Required Documents**:
- Articles of Incorporation / Certificate of Incorporation
- Bylaws / Operating Agreement
- Initial Board Resolutions
- Registered Agent Appointment

---

### Step 4: File with Government Authority

**Filing Requirements**:
- Submit incorporation documents
- Pay filing fees
- Provide required information
- Obtain filing confirmation

---

### Step 5: Obtain Certificate

**After Filing**:
- Receive Certificate of Incorporation
- Verify information accuracy
- Store securely
- Update company records
