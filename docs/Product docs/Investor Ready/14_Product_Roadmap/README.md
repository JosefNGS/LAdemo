# 14_Product_Roadmap

## Purpose
This folder contains future feature lists, R&D plans, and product development timeline.

## Required Documents

### Product Roadmap
- **Status**: ⚠️ To be created
- **Purpose**: Future feature list and development plans
- **Content**: 
  - Feature roadmap (next 4-6 quarters)
  - Product priorities
  - New product lines
  - Platform enhancements
- **Usage**: Show product vision and development plans

### R&D Plans
- **Status**: ⚠️ To be created
- **Purpose**: Research and development initiatives
- **Content**: 
  - R&D priorities
  - Technology research
  - Innovation initiatives
  - Experimental features
- **Usage**: Demonstrate commitment to innovation

### Development Timeline
- **Status**: ⚠️ To be created
- **Purpose**: Timeline for next 4–6 quarters
- **Content**: 
  - Quarterly milestones
  - Feature release schedule
  - Major product launches
  - Platform updates
- **Usage**: Show execution plan and timeline

## Roadmap Categories
- **Core Platform**: Essential features and improvements
- **New Products**: Additional product lines or services
- **Integrations**: Third-party integrations and partnerships
- **Infrastructure**: Technical improvements and scalability
- **User Experience**: UX/UI enhancements and optimizations

## Notes
- Roadmap should be realistic and achievable
- Include both short-term (next quarter) and long-term (6+ months) plans
- Show alignment with business goals and market needs
- Update regularly as priorities and market conditions change
