## Model Structure

### Tab 1: Assumptions

**Key Variables**:
- User growth rates (monthly/quarterly/annual)
- Conversion rates (visitor to user, free to paid)
- Pricing assumptions (tiers, discounts, enterprise)
- Revenue share percentages
- Cost assumptions (CAC, infrastructure, personnel)
- Market penetration rates
- Churn rates
- Retention rates

**Growth Assumptions**:
- Year 1: 10,000 active users
- Year 2: 50,000 active users
- Year 3: 100,000+ active users

**Revenue Assumptions**:
- Affiliate platform fees: 20% of commissions
- Staking management fees: 10% of staking rewards
- MEV redistribution: 5% of MEV capture
- Premium subscriptions: $35/month (Pro), $249/month (Enterprise)

**Cost Assumptions**:
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): $50-100
- Infrastructure costs: $5-10 per user/month
- Personnel costs: See Salary Breakdown document
- Marketing costs: 20-30% of revenue

## Scenario Analysis

### Base Case Scenario

**Assumptions**:
- Moderate user growth
- Standard conversion rates
- Average pricing
- Normal market conditions

**Projections**:
- Year 1: $500K - $1M revenue
- Year 2: $5M - $10M revenue
- Year 3: $15M - $25M revenue

### Bull Case Scenario

**Assumptions**:
- High user growth
- Above-average conversion rates
- Premium pricing
- Favorable market conditions

**Projections**:
- Year 1: $1M - $2M revenue
- Year 2: $10M - $20M revenue
- Year 3: $25M - $50M revenue

### Bear Case Scenario

**Assumptions**:
- Slow user growth
- Below-average conversion rates
- Competitive pricing pressure
- Challenging market conditions

**Projections**:
- Year 1: $250K - $500K revenue
- Year 2: $2M - $5M revenue
- Year 3: $8M - $15M revenue

## Notes & Assumptions

### Revenue Recognition
- Affiliate fees: Recognized when commissions are paid to users
- Staking fees: Recognized monthly based on staking activity
- MEV fees: Recognized when MEV is captured
- Subscriptions: Recognized monthly

### Cost Recognition
- Infrastructure: Variable costs based on usage
- Personnel: Fixed costs with planned increases
- Marketing: Variable costs based on growth targets
- Development: Mix of fixed and variable costs

### Accounting Standards
- Follow GAAP (Generally Accepted Accounting Principles)
- Revenue recognition per ASC 606
- Expense recognition on accrual basis
