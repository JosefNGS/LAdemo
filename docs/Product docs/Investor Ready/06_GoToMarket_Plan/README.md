# 06_GoToMarket_Plan

## Purpose
This folder contains marketing strategy, sales processes, and competitive analysis.

## Required Documents

### Marketing Strategy
- **Status**: ⚠️ To be created
- **Purpose**: Channels, CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) estimates, and budget
- **Content**: 
  - Marketing channels and tactics
  - Customer acquisition strategy
  - CAC estimates and LTV (Lifetime Value) analysis
  - Marketing budget allocation
  - Growth marketing plans
- **Usage**: Demonstrate how you'll acquire customers

### Sales Playbook
- **Status**: ⚠️ To be created
- **Purpose**: How the sales team closes deals (scripts, process)
- **Content**: 
  - Sales process and methodology
  - Sales scripts and talking points
  - Objection handling
  - Pricing and negotiation guidelines
  - Sales team structure
- **Usage**: Show sales execution capability

### Competitor Analysis
- **Status**: ✅ Available as `Competitor_Analysis.md`
- **Purpose**: Feature matrix comparing you to the market
- **Content**: 
  - Competitive landscape overview
  - Feature comparison matrix
  - Competitive advantages
  - Market positioning
- **Usage**: Demonstrate market understanding and differentiation

### Market Strategy
- **Status**: ✅ Available as `Market_Strategy.md`
- **Purpose**: Strategic market analysis and positioning
- **Content**: 
  - SWOT analysis
  - Market opportunities
  - Target market segments
  - Go-to-market approach
- **Usage**: Show strategic thinking and market understanding

## Notes
- Marketing strategy should include specific, measurable tactics
- Sales playbook should be actionable and tested
- Competitor analysis should be honest and comprehensive
- All documents should be updated as market conditions change
