## Product Grading System

### Grade-Based Ranking System

**Implementation**: Products are graded using a comprehensive 12-level system (S, A+, A, A-, B+, B, B-, C+, C, C-, D, F) and displayed in the marketplace sorted by grade.

#### Grade Levels

- **S Grade** (Exceptional): Highest quality products, premium features
- **A+ Grade** (Excellent): Outstanding products with superior value
- **A Grade** (Very Good): High-quality products with strong value proposition
- **A- Grade** (Good): Good products meeting quality standards
- **B+ Grade** (Above Average): Products above average quality
- **B Grade** (Average): Standard quality products
- **B- Grade** (Below Average): Minimum acceptable quality (included in catalog)
- **C+ Grade** (Fair): Below standards, not featured in main catalog
- **C Grade** (Poor): Significant quality issues
- **C- Grade** (Very Poor): Multiple quality concerns
- **D Grade** (Failing): Failed to meet basic standards
- **F Grade** (Unacceptable): Rejected from marketplace

#### Grading Criteria

Products are evaluated based on:
1. **Quality**: Product build, features, user experience
2. **Security**: Security features and vulnerability assessment (if applicable)
3. **Value Proposition**: Benefit-to-cost ratio, ROI potential
4. **Affiliate Potential**: Commission structure, conversion likelihood
5. **Market Demand**: User interest, search volume, market trends
6. **User Reviews**: Community feedback and ratings
7. **Compliance**: Platform policies, legal requirements, terms adherence

#### Catalog Inclusion Policy

- **B- or Higher**: Featured in main marketplace catalog
- **C+ or Lower**: Not featured (may be available through direct links)
- **D/F Grades**: Rejected and removed from platform

**Visual Display**: Grade cards with color-coded gradients visible in Due Diligence section

**Accessibility**: Marketplace ‚Üí Due Diligence tab ‚Üí "Product Ranking System"

### Grading System Benefits

- **Quality Transparency**: Users can see product quality at a glance
- **Informed Selection**: Higher grades indicate better products
- **User Protection**: Low-quality products filtered out
- **Vendor Accountability**: Vendors know grading criteria and standards
- **Platform Credibility**: Demonstrates commitment to quality
